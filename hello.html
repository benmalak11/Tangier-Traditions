Rich Heritage

For centuries, Tangier has been a crossroads of cultures. This history lives on in the clothing we proudly present. Each caftan is not just fabric — it’s a story woven with care by artisans whose skills have been passed down through generations.

Craftsmanship & Materials

Our collection features authentic designs made with the finest materials: silks, satins, and delicate hand-embroidered details. Many pieces take weeks to complete, showing the dedication and artistry of Moroccan craftsmanship.
